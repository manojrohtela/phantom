Refund Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026 · Phantom is an AgentHive product.

Try before you buy

Phantom includes a free 3-day trial so you can fully evaluate it on your own device before paying. We strongly encourage you to use the trial to confirm Phantom works for you.

After purchase

Because a Phantom license is a digital product delivered as a license key, and a free trial is available beforehand, license purchases are generally non-refundable once a key has been issued.

When we will refund

We'll issue a full refund if:

  • you were charged but never received a working key, and we can't resolve it; or
  • you were charged more than once for the same order; or
  • a refund is required by applicable consumer law.

How to request

Email support@heyagenthive.com from the address on your order, within 7 days of purchase, with your UPI payment reference. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method.
