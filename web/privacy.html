Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026 · Phantom is an AgentHive product.

This policy covers Phantom specifically and works alongside the AgentHive Privacy Policy. Phantom is built to be privacy-first: your conversations stay on your device.

Your audio and answers stay local. Phantom captures your microphone and sends it, with your own AI provider key, directly from your device to the AI provider you chose. Your audio, transcripts, and answers do not pass through and are not stored on our servers. Your API keys are stored only on your device.

1. What we collect

  • Order data — name, email, phone, and a UPI payment reference when you buy a license, so we can verify payment and send your key.
  • License & device data — a non-reversible device identifier used only to run the trial fairly and bind a key to one device at a time.

2. What we never receive

  • Your microphone audio, transcripts, screen contents, or AI answers.
  • Your AI provider API keys.

3. How we use order data

Only to verify payment, issue and manage your license, provide support, and meet legal/tax obligations. We do not sell your data.

4. Third parties

We use hosting and email providers to operate, and you connect the AI provider of your choice. Each AI provider's handling of the data you send it is governed by that provider's own privacy policy.

5. Retention & your rights

We keep order and license data while your license is active and as required for legal/tax purposes. You may request access to or deletion of the data we hold by emailing us. We handle personal data consistent with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

support@heyagenthive.com
